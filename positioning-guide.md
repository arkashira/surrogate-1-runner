
# Positioning Guide for Micro-SaaS Founders

This guide aims to help you position your product effectively, enabling you to communicate its value to your target audience.

## Comprehensive and Easy to Follow

This guide provides a clear, actionable roadmap to positioning your product.

## Includes Examples and Case Studies

Real-world examples and case studies are included to illustrate effective positioning strategies.

## Integrated with the Onboarding Process

This guide is integrated with the onboarding process, ensuring that you have access to it as soon as you start using the product.
