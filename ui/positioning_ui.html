Product Positioning Guide

Understand Your Market

Identify your target audience and their needs. Research your competitors and find your unique value proposition.

Case Study: SaaS Startup

This startup identified a niche in the market by focusing on a specific pain point their competitors overlooked.

Define Your Value Proposition

Clearly articulate what makes your product unique and why customers should choose you over competitors.

Example: Unique Features

Highlight the key features that set your product apart, such as ease of use, advanced analytics, or superior customer support.

Craft Your Messaging

Develop clear and consistent messaging that resonates with your target audience. Use language that speaks to their needs and desires.

Example: Messaging Framework

Create a messaging framework that includes your value proposition, key benefits, and supporting points.
