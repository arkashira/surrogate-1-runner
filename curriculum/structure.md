
# English Communication Skills for IT Professionals

## Module 1: Foundational English for IT

- **Unit 1.1: Basic Grammar and Vocabulary**
  - Interactive exercises to improve understanding of fundamental grammar rules and expand vocabulary relevant to IT.

- **Unit 1.2: Listening and Speaking**
  - Listening comprehension exercises focused on IT-related topics.
  - Speaking activities to build confidence in expressing ideas and opinions.

## Module 2: Effective Communication in IT Teams

- **Unit 2.1: Written Communication**
  - Email and chat etiquette for IT professionals.
  - Writing clear and concise technical documents.

- **Unit 2.2: Verbal Communication**
  - Active listening skills in IT team settings.
  - Presenting technical ideas effectively.

## Module 3: Advanced English for IT Leadership

- **Unit 3.1: Negotiation and Persuasion**
  - Strategies for negotiating technical solutions and resources.
  - Persuading stakeholders through clear and compelling arguments.

- **Unit 3.2: Leadership and Management**
  - Communicating vision and goals to IT teams.
  - Providing constructive feedback and mentoring team members.

## Assessment

- A final assessment will be conducted to evaluate the learner's proficiency in English communication skills for IT professionals.
- The assessment will cover all units and include a mix of multiple-choice questions, fill-in-the-blank, and scenario-based tasks.

## Summary

- The curriculum covers essential English communication skills for IT professionals, structured in a logical and easy-to-follow manner.
- It includes interactive exercises and assessments to enhance learning and reinforce understanding.
- The curriculum is designed to cater to IT professionals at different levels, from foundational to advanced communication skills.
