## We Value Your Input
Your feedback is crucial for us to improve and tailor our products to your needs. Please take a moment to share your experience with the Axentx Surrogate-1 sensor.

## Net Promoter Score (NPS)
We use the Net Promoter Score (NPS) to measure customer loyalty. NPS is calculated based on the following question:

**How likely are you to recommend Axentx Surrogate-1 to a friend or colleague?**

Please rate your likelihood to recommend on a scale from 0 to 10, where 0 is "Not at all likely" and 10 is "Extremely likely."
