# Engagement Stage Guide

## Overview
During **Engagement**, prospects who discovered your product are now evaluating it. The aim is to build trust, demonstrate value, and move them closer to a decision.

## Key Objectives
1. **Capture leads** – Offer gated assets (e‑books, webinars).  
2. **Nurture via email** – Automated drip campaigns that educate and address objections.  
3. **Show product in action** – Interactive demos, case studies, and free trials.

## Tactics & Examples

| Tactic | Description | Real‑World Example |
|--------|-------------|--------------------|
| **Lead Magnet (e‑book)** | Create a downloadable guide that solves a specific problem. Require email to access. | “The Ultimate Guide to Scalable Data Pipelines” – 5,000 downloads in 2 weeks. |
| **Drip Email Sequence** | 5‑email series: welcome → problem → solution → case study → CTA. | Email 3 includes a short video demo of the surrogate‑1 pipeline, boosting trial sign‑ups by 30%. |
| **Live Webinar** | Host a 30‑minute live demo with Q&A. Record and repurpose. | Webinar “From Raw Data to Insight in 15 Minutes” converts 12% of attendees to paid users. |

## Metrics to Track
- **Conversion Rate (lead capture)** – Visitors → leads.  
- **Email Open & Reply Rates** – Engagement with nurture content.  
- **Demo/Trial Sign‑Ups** – Number of users who start a free trial.

## Checklist
- [ ] Lead magnet created and integrated with a landing page.  
- [ ] Email automation workflow built in your ESP (e.g., Mailchimp, SendGrid).  
- [ ] Webinar date scheduled and promotion plan executed.
