# Conversion Stage Guide

## Overview
The **Conversion** stage turns engaged prospects into paying customers. Focus on reducing friction, reinforcing value, and providing a clear path to purchase.

## Key Objectives
1. **Simplify checkout** – Minimal steps, clear pricing, and multiple payment options.  
2. **Offer risk‑reduction** – Free trial, money‑back guarantee, or limited‑time discount.  
3. **Leverage social proof** – Testimonials, case studies, and trust badges.

## Tactics & Examples

| Tactic | Description | Real‑World Example |
|--------|-------------|--------------------|
| **One‑Click Checkout** | Pre‑fill known fields, support Apple Pay/Google Pay. | Checkout page reduces steps from 5 to 2, decreasing cart abandonment by 18%. |
| **30‑Day Money‑Back Guarantee** | Remove purchase anxiety with a clear refund policy. | Guarantees posted prominently; support tickets drop by 22%. |
| **Customer Success Stories** | Embed short video testimonials on pricing page. | “How Acme Corp cut data processing time by 70%” video boosts conversion by 12%. |

## Metrics to Track
- **Cart Abandonment Rate** – % of users who start checkout but don’t complete.  
- **Conversion Rate** – Leads → paying customers.  
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)** – Revenue ÷ total customers.
