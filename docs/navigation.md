## Growth Stages

### Stage 1 – Ideation & Validation
- **Focus:** Market research, problem‑solution fit, early user interviews.
- **Key Tactics:** Landing‑page tests, pre‑launch email capture, community outreach.

### Stage 2 – Launch & Early Traction
- **Focus:** Product‑market fit, initial user acquisition.
- **Key Tactics:** Content marketing, paid ads, beta program, early‑adopter incentives.

### Stage 3 – Scaling & Retention
- **Focus:** Sustainable growth, churn reduction.
- **Key Tactics:** On‑boarding flows, email drip campaigns, referral programs, analytics‑driven optimization.

### Stage 4 – Maturity & Expansion
- **Focus:** Market expansion, upsells, ecosystem building.
- **Key Tactics:** Partnerships, API integrations, enterprise sales, brand advocacy.

## Actionable Tactics

### Acquisition
- SEO‑optimized blog posts
- Targeted social ads
- Guest podcast appearances

### Activation
- Interactive product tours
- In‑app onboarding checklists
- First‑value triggers (e.g., “quick win” feature)

### Retention
- Automated re‑engagement emails
- Usage‑based nudges
- Community forums & AMA sessions

### Revenue
- Tiered pricing experiments
- Limited‑time promotions
- Bundled feature packages

### Referral
- Double‑sided referral rewards
- Shareable “invite‑a‑friend” links
- Social proof widgets
