## 1. Acceptance Criteria  

- **Traffic Weight Update** – After a deployment, the Istio VirtualService `http` route `weight` fields must reflect the new traffic split exactly as specified in the deployment config.  
- **Health Verification** – The `TrafficVerifier` must call the configured health endpoint and return `PASS` only when the success rate ≥ the configured threshold; otherwise `FAIL`.  
- **Rollback Trigger** – If health verification returns `FAIL` and `rollback_on_failure=True`, the pipeline must revert the VirtualService to its pre‑deployment traffic weights and mark the deployment status as `ROLLED_BACK`.  
- **Canary Increment** – For a canary strategy, the traffic weight for the new version must increase by `weight_increment` every `increment_interval_seconds` until it reaches `target_weight`.  
- **Blue‑Green Atomic Shift** – For a blue‑green strategy, the traffic must switch from 100% old to 100% new in a single atomic update (no intermediate weights).  
- **Deployment Status** – The final deployment result must be one of `SUCCESS`, `FAILED`, or `ROLLED_BACK`, accompanied by a descriptive message.
