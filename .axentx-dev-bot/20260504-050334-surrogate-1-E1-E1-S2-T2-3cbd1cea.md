# Connecting Your SaaS to AxentX Surrogate-1

This comprehensive guide synthesizes the best practices from multiple AI proposals to ensure a seamless integration of your SaaS platform with AxentX Surrogate-1. ## Overview

Connecting your SaaS platform to AxentX Surrogate-1 involves several straightforward steps:

1. **Registering your SaaS** – Create a new SaaS entry in the AxentX portal.
2. **Authenticating** – Provide OAuth tokens or API keys for secure data access.
3. **Storing Data** – Your SaaS data is encrypted at rest and stored securely.
4. **Receiving Advice** – Once connected, the system generates and delivers your first marketing recommendation within 24 hours.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active **AxentX account** with access to the Surrogate-1 dashboard.
- Administrative rights on the SaaS platform you wish to connect (e.g., Shopify, HubSpot, Salesforce).
- Ability to create **API tokens** or **OAuth credentials** on your SaaS.

---

## Step-by-Step Connection Flow

### 1. Register Your SaaS

1. Log in to the AxentX dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Settings → SaaS Connections**.
3. Click **Add New SaaS** and fill in:
   - **Name** – A friendly name for your SaaS.
   - **Type** – Select the SaaS platform (e.g., Shopify, HubSpot, Salesforce).
   - **Description** – Optional details.

### 2. Authenticate

Depending on the SaaS type, follow these instructions:

| SaaS | Authentication Method | Instructions |
|------|-----------------------|--------------|
| Shopify | OAuth 2.0 | Follow the OAuth flow in the dashboard. |
| HubSpot | API Key | Paste your HubSpot API key. |
| Salesforce | OAuth 2.0 | Use the Salesforce OAuth wizard. |

> **Security Note**: All credentials are encrypted using AES-256 before storage.

### 3. Verify Connection

After authentication, the dashboard will display a **Connection Status** indicator. A green checkmark means the connection is live. If it shows red, review the error message and re-authenticate.

---

## Data Security & Storage

- **Encryption at Rest:** All SaaS data is encrypted using AES-256 before being written to the central data lake.
- **Encryption in Transit:** Connections use HTTPS/TLS 1.3; API tokens are never logged.
- **Access Controls:** Only the Surrogate-1 service account can read the stored data. No other internal services have direct access without explicit permission.
- **Retention Policy:** Raw SaaS data is retained for **30 days**; derived insights are stored indefinitely per your subscription tier.

---

## Receiving Your First Marketing Advice

1. After a successful connection, the system ingests your SaaS data every 30 minutes.
2. It processes the data through our recommendation engine.
3. The first tailored marketing advice is sent to your email and displayed in the **Insights** tab within 24 hours.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Symptom | Fix |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Connection fails | Status shows red | Re-authenticate or check API limits |
| No advice received | No email or Insights tab empty | Ensure data ingestion succeeded (check logs) |
| Data not updated | Old data appears | Wait up to 30 minutes for the next ingestion cycle |
| Invalid token | Token expired or mistyped | Regenerate a fresh token and re-enter it |
| No data appears after ingestion | Insufficient scopes on token | Ensure the token includes read access to required
