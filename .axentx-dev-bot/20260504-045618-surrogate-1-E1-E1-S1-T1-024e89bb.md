## 1. Acceptance Criteria
- **Automatic Appearance** – The wizard modal must render immediately after the first successful login when `localStorage.getItem('onboardingDone')` is `null` or `false`.
- **Step Content & Visual Cues** – Each step displays its title, description, and a visual indicator (e.g., icon or progress bar) that matches the design spec.
- **Navigation Controls** – Users can:
  - Click **Next** to advance to the following step.
  - Click **Back** to return to the previous step (except on the first step).
  - Click **Skip** to bypass the current step; a banner reminds them to return later.
  - Click **Close** to exit the wizard; the wizard remains hidden until the next login.
- **Progress Tracking** – A progress bar or step counter accurately reflects the current step number out of the total steps.
- **Completion Handling** – When the final step is finished:
  - `localStorage.setItem('onboardingDone', 'true')` is called.
  - An optional `onComplete` callback prop is invoked.
- **Accessibility** – All interactive elements are focusable, have ARIA labels, and the modal traps focus while open.
- **Responsiveness** – The wizard layout adapts to viewport widths ≥320px and ≤1440px without horizontal scrolling.
