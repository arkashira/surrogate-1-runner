## 1. Acceptance Criteria

- **AC1 – Controlled Traffic Shifts**  
  The `IstioTrafficShifter` must apply traffic weights in the sequence `[20, 40, 60, 80, 100]` percent to the new revision while maintaining the remainder on the old revision.

- **AC2 – Health Checks Before Shift**  
  Before each weight change, `IstioHealthChecker` must perform:
  1. HTTP readiness probe on the new revision’s pod(s).
  2. Kubernetes pod status check (Running & Ready).
  3. Optional Istio sidecar health endpoint (`/healthz`) if enabled.

- **AC3 – Automatic Rollback**  
  If any health check fails, the shifter must immediately revert to the previous weight and stop further shifting.

- **AC4 – Retry & Timeout Logic**  
  Health checks must retry up to `max_retries` (default 3) with a `timeout` (default 5 s) per attempt. Failures after retries trigger rollback.

- **AC5 – Logging & Observability**  
  Each shift step, health check result, retry attempt, and rollback must be logged with sufficient detail (timestamp, weight, pod name, status).

- **AC6 – Testability**  
  External calls (`kubectl`, `istioctl`, HTTP requests) must be injectable so unit tests can mock them.
