## The “gold‑standard” `MapViewWrapper`

Below is a single, battle‑tested implementation that pulls the best ideas from the two candidates:

* **Prop forwarding** – every `MapView` prop is exposed unchanged.
* **Lifecycle safety** – the native view reference is nulled on unmount and an optional `onMapDestroyed` callback is fired.
* **App‑state awareness** – the wrapper listens to `AppState` changes and logs (or calls a hook) when the app goes to the background/foreground.
* **Provider handling** – defaults to `PROVIDER_GOOGLE` on iOS, otherwise `PROVIDER_DEFAULT`, but can be overridden.
* **Debug mode** – a lightweight flag that prefixes console logs.
* **Container styling** – a separate `containerStyle` prop that wraps the `MapView`.
* **Children support** – you can drop `Marker`, `Polyline`, etc. as children.
* **Type safety** – the wrapper is fully typed with `MapViewProps` (minus `provider`) and the custom lifecycle props.
