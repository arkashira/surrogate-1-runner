- **Latency**: An alert is dispatched **≤ 60 seconds** after a background job failure is detected.

- **Delivery**: The alert is successfully sent to the **configured notification channel** (email or Slack) as specified in `config.yaml`.  
- **Retry**: On transient send failures, the notifier retries **up to 3 times** with an exponential back‑off (initial 2 s, doubling each attempt).  
- **Observability**: A health‑check endpoint (`/health`) returns `200 OK` when the notifier is operational and `503` otherwise.
