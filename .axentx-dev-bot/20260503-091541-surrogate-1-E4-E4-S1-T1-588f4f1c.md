REJECT: Critical data/session bug and unsafe retry loop — `datetime` not imported, hardcoded DB credentials, recursive retry can overflow, and infinite loop in web process will block the app.

- Missing `import datetime` in `enqueue_notification` will cause runtime NameError on status change.  
- Hardcoded `postgresql://user:password@host:port/dbname` risks secret leakage and won’t work across environments.  
- Recursive `deliver_notification` on exception can blow the stack under repeated failures; no exponential backoff or max retries.

- No transaction/session safety in retry path and `process_outbox` may reprocess or lose items on crash.

Acceptable next step would be to: import `datetime`, move DB URL to config/env, cap retries with backoff, and defer background processing to a worker (or at least a thread-safe scheduler) instead of a blocking loop in the web route.
